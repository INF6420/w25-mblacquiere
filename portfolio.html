Public Library's Role in Achieving Digital Equity Through Inclusion


This essay I wrote for INF7050 looks at the role libraries play in providing access to information and helping support the development of digital literacy skills that will assist people in navigating the modern world successfully.


Social Impact of Home Library Service on Older Adults


This research proposal, prepared for INF7996, proposes a study that would explore the impact of home library services on older adults who are at risk of social isolation and loneliness. In 2021 the World Health Organization published an advocacy briefing on social isolation and loneliness among older people and in 2024 established a WHO Commission on Social Connection that aims to see the issue recognized and resourced as a global public health priority. I hope to have the opportunity to explore this topic, and the impact libraries can make, more in the future.
